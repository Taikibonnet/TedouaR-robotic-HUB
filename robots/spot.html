Spot

Quadruped
Boston Dynamics
Quadruped Boston Dynamics Mobile Robot Inspection Industrial

Spot is an agile mobile robot designed for sensing, inspection, and remote operation. Customizable for diverse applications, from industrial inspections to public safety.

Technical Specifications

Height 33 inches (0.84 meters)
Weight 71 pounds (32.2 kg)
Payload 14 kg (standard), up to 25 kg with additional equipment
Battery Life 90 minutes (typical mission)
Speed Up to 1.6 m/s (5.76 km/h)
Degrees of Freedom 12 (3 per leg)
Sensors Stereo cameras, depth cameras, IMU, position/force sensors
Dust/Water Resistance IP54 rated
Operating Temperature -20° to 45°C

Overview

Spot is a four-legged robot developed by Boston Dynamics that's designed to navigate terrain that would be challenging for wheeled robots. With its advanced mobility, Spot can climb stairs, traverse rough ground, and maintain stability in various environments. Originally introduced in 2016, Spot became commercially available in 2020, marking a significant milestone in deploying advanced robotics for practical applications.

The robot features a distinctive yellow-black design with an articulated "head" that contains a suite of sensors for navigating and mapping environments. Its adaptable platform allows for the addition of numerous payloads to customize it for specific tasks, from data collection to physical manipulation using an optional robotic arm attachment.

Capabilities

Spot's capabilities make it valuable across multiple industries:

  • Mobility: Can navigate stairs, rough terrain, and inclines up to 30 degrees
  • Autonomy: Features autonomous navigation with obstacle avoidance
  • Mapping: Creates detailed 3D maps of environments
  • Manipulation: Optional robotic arm allows for object manipulation and interaction
  • Data Collection: Multiple camera options including 360° cameras, thermal imaging, gas detection
  • Programmability: SDK allows for custom application development
  • Remote Operation: Can be teleoperated from a distance via tablet or computer interface

Applications

Spot has been deployed in numerous industries for various applications:

  • Industrial Inspection: Monitoring of equipment, gauges, and facilities in factories, power plants, and construction sites
  • Energy: Remote data collection in challenging environments like offshore oil platforms
  • Construction: Site documentation, progress monitoring, and safety inspections
  • Public Safety: Remote assessment of hazardous situations
  • Research: Platform for developing and testing new robotics technologies
  • Entertainment: Performances and demonstrations showcasing robotic capabilities
