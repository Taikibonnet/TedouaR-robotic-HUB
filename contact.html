Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contribute to the encyclopedia?

To contribute to our encyclopedia, you need to create an account and submit your content through our contribution form. All submissions are reviewed by our editorial team before being published. We welcome detailed information about robots, technologies, and applications that aren't yet featured on our platform.

Is there a mobile app for TedouaR Robotics Hub?

We're currently developing mobile applications for iOS and Android. They're expected to launch in late 2025. In the meantime, our website is fully responsive and works well on mobile devices.

How do I report an error or inaccuracy?

If you find an error or inaccuracy in our content, please use the "Report an Issue" button available on each robot's detail page, or contact us directly via the form on this page. We appreciate your help in maintaining the accuracy of our information.

Do you offer educational resources for schools?

Yes, we offer educational packages for schools, including lesson plans, guided activities, and specialized content designed for different age groups. Please contact us with details about your educational needs for more information.

Can I use content from the encyclopedia in my research or publications?

Our content is available for educational and research purposes under a Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial license. This means you can use our content in non-commercial projects as long as you provide proper attribution to TedouaR Robotics Hub. For commercial uses, please contact us for permission.

How can companies partner with TedouaR Robotics Hub?

We're open to partnerships with companies and organizations in the robotics industry. Partnerships can include content collaboration, technical validation, educational initiatives, and more. Please contact us with your proposal, and our partnership team will get back to you.
